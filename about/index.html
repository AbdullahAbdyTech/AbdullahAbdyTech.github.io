About

AI Course Map helps learners choose a practical route into AI.

AI Course Map is an independent educational directory. We organize AI course ideas, roadmaps, and original study guides so beginners can compare learning paths without opening dozens of tabs.

How we publish

Our articles are written as educational planning guides, not as copied course pages. We focus on what a learner should understand before choosing a course: prerequisites, practice tasks, certificate tradeoffs, project ideas, and signs that a course is worth the time.

Course prices, certificates, duration, and availability can change on provider websites. Readers should always confirm final details on the original source before enrolling or paying for a certificate.
